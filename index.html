WEB에 대하여

It is usually called the World Wide Web. Unlike the existing information service that provided only text as the information retrieval service, it supports pictures, moving pictures, and sound.




In addition, it introduces the concept of hypertext, which makes it easy to find information related to the desired information. Ease of use as a graphical environment is one of the reasons for the rapid rise of the Internet.
